# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Codex desktop users who keep a small quota panel visible while working. They need to read quota and activity data at a glance without leaving their current task.

## Product Purpose

Show current Codex usage in a compact floating panel, with optional weather scenes that make the panel pleasant to keep on screen without reducing data clarity.

## Brand Personality

Calm, atmospheric, precise.

## Anti-references

Avoid mismatched scenery, decorative particles with no clear physical meaning, excessive glow, toy-like effects, busy motion, and backgrounds that compete with quota data.

## Design Principles

- Data remains readable before visual atmosphere.
- Every weather effect must clearly belong to its scene.
- Backgrounds within one theme share a coherent mood and color range.
- Motion should feel natural, layered, and restrained.
- Manual and automatic transitions must remain reliable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain strong text contrast and respect reduced-motion preferences by lowering particle count and motion speed.
